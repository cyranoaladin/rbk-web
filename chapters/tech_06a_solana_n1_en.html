W01 Thinking in Rust

Algorithms & Types

  • Immutability by Default: Why Rust refuses `let x = 5; x = 6;`.
  • Type System: Structs, Enums, and powerful Pattern Matching (`match`).
  • Option & Result: Error handling without `null` or exceptions.
"90% of Solana bugs come from poor understanding of Rust types."

W02 Conquering Memory

Ownership & Borrowing

  • The Borrow Checker: Rules of unique ownership and multiple borrowing.
  • Lifetimes: `'a` annotations to guarantee reference validity.
  • Traits & Generics: Static polymorphism (Zero-cost abstraction).
"If it compiles, it works. The Rust compiler is your first mentor."

W03 Solana Theory

Architecture & Cryptography

  • Account Model: Everything is an account (Data, Executable, System).
  • PDAs (Program Derived Addresses): Deterministic derivation off the Ed25519 curve.
  • Runtime: Sealevel, Pipelining, and transaction parallelization.

How to read the program: Phases vs Levels

Engineering = 16 weeks: W01–W16. Phases = Hardening (W01–W04), Building (W05–W10), Security & Audit (W11–W14), Capstone (W15–W16). Levels = N1 (W01–W04), N2 (W05–W10), N3 (W11–W16). There is no week beyond W16 in this program.
